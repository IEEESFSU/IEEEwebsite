Soldering Workshop

When:
10/16/2019
3:00pm-4:00pm
Where:
SCI 111
Cost:
$10 for IEEE Members, $15 for non-IEEE members

Event Details: Led by Chris Piland, an SFSU senior and EE major, this soldering workshop will teach you the basics of soldering and the different applications one may encounter. If you have little to no soldering experience, this event is perfect for your beginning steps to improving your technical prowess. If you have a large amount of experience, this event will teach you how to solder to military specification over a wide range of soldering styles, multi conductor wires, through hole components, and surface mount components. We encourage anyone, not just Electrical and Computer Engineers, to attend with a willingness and a desire to learn!
